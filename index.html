Our Story

M.A.K.A.N. is a digital Pay-It-Forward initiative that allows donors to sponsor meals for the elderly and low-income families, redeemable at local hawkers. We bridge community generosity with hawker sustainability using smart, inclusive technology.

What We Offer

  • Seamless donation of meals via app or QR code
  • Transparent tracking of donations and redemptions
  • Support for local hawkers through steady business
  • Beneficiary-friendly QR redemption with dignity

Benefits

✔ Easy onboarding for hawkers
✔ Mobile-optimized donor experience
✔ Secure and low-fee payment system
✔ Real-time insights for administrators
